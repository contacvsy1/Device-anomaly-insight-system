Objective

Detect abnormal disk utilization and convert it into clear operational insight

The project demonstrates a compact anomaly detection system for streaming or device telemetry, focused on disk utilization behavior.

Primary Goals

Find unusual DU behavior

Identify rows where disk utilization is unusual compared with hour, ad load, DMA, device type, and content context.

Explain why it matters

Add readable explanations such as high ad load, peak hour usage, critical utilization, or injected spike pattern.

Recommend next action

Turn anomaly rows into actionable operations guidance for investigation, cleanup, cache purge, or trend monitoring.

Business Interpretation

Disk utilization anomalies can indicate device storage pressure, workload spikes, content delivery stress, or abnormal behavior during peak viewing windows. The output helps an operations or analytics team prioritize which devices or usage contexts deserve attention.
